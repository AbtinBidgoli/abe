Abstract

Michigan born, Tobi Tobin is a Los Angeles based Lifestyle Expert, whose work has graced the cover of Architectural Digest & earned her the AIA award. Designing environments for some of the most powerful & influential people in Hollywood as well as abroad. Tobi Tobin injects fifteen years of experience into the public eye by launching her Tobi Tobin Luxury Collection that consists of candles, chocolates & fragrances. Tobi Tobin’s brand is the story of architecture & place. Her candles are all named after historical periods & the types of architecture that have inspired her interior design career for years. Her fragrances tell the story of her early travels around the world as a model. Tobin’s clients & customers include Keanu Reeves, Taylor Swift, Reese Witherspoon, Renee Zellweger, Sean Combs, Robert Downey Jr, Chelsea Handler, Ryan Tedder & Leonardo DiCaprio. Tobi’s passion & perfection is evident in all of her collections. Her purpose & focus is impacting individuals aspiring to live an affordable luxury clean lifestyle.
