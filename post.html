The Perfect Landing Page Structure

  • Abtin Bidgoli
  • 14 June 2021
  • 5 minute read

The first interaction that your organization has with a customer is a chance to make a great & lasting impression. More importantly, it's your first impression. That warrants a lot of your attention since you only get one.

There’s 3 parts to a landing page: what’s immediately visible (loading screen), hero section header (above the fold) & what the user scrolls to (below the fold). Not blowing this impression means following a de-risked board plan.
