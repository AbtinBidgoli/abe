Bespoke Process

01

Brief

To improve customer experience, organizations can choose solutions ranging from low to high investment & impact, depending on your current state, budget, risk tolerance, transformation readiness, & unmet needs. Best practices starts with an Initial Discovery, Creative Strategy & Intuitive Wireframing briefs to collect & consolidate your data.

02

Design

Responsive design creates a single site to support many devices, but need to consider content, design & performance across devices to ensure usability. I may use visuals to indicate a creative direction, but ultimately you have full creative control. Testing early design ideas lets you fix usability problems before you waste money implementing something that doesn't work.

03

Develop

Based on the approved design strategy, the purpose of this objective is converting the design concept into Web pages. I will create HTML & CSS page templates for the main & basic interior pages. I develop pages iteratively that adapt to the capabilities of many devices & screen sizes, so we won’t waste time mocking up every template as a static visual.

04

Publish

Configure your DNS records, DNS stands for Domain Name System. This system is essentially the phone book of the Web that organizes & identifies domains. While a phone book translates a name like "com.abtinbidgoli" into the correct phone number to call, the DNS translates a web address like "abtinbidgoli.com" into the physical IP address.
